Fall 2023 I took professor Toma's advanced algorithms GIS course. In the course I worked on a series of projectst that visual representations of viewshed, sea-level rise, drainage & flooding, contour elevation, aspect ratio, Hillshading, contour lines.

Sea Level Rise Uses a queue of boundary sea points to incrementally flood the land. Shown below is a map of North Truro and Provincetown MA. (the outermost points of cape cod). This code helps visualize flooding in a meaniful way, if configured to flood with the incrementally simulating slr every ten years can make meaning ful depictions regarding climate change and seal level rise. To better depict the results the flooding results are overlayed ontop of the hillshade.
